What is Vijay Diwas

On 26 July 1999, Indian forces recaptured the last Pakistani-held peak in Kargil. Operation Vijay was complete. 527 soldiers had given their lives. India calls it Kargil Vijay Diwas — Victory Day.

Regiment is a game built around these battles. Every unit is a real soldier. Every battle is a real engagement. You can play it any day — but 26 July is the day it was made for.
