What is Regiment?

Regiment is not a war simulator. It is a memorial disguised as a game — each hex on the grid is a real soldier with a biography, a regiment, and often a death. The gameplay makes you feel what they faced, not just read about it.

Most war games abstract soldiers into units. Regiment inverts that. When you go home, tell them of us and say — for your tomorrow, we gave our today.
